Position Overview

 

We are seeking a skilled and experienced Frontend Software Engineer to join our team on a full-time basis. As a Frontend Software Engineer at DummyNode, you will be responsible for designing and implementing user interfaces for web and mobile applications. This role offers the opportunity to work on exciting projects, collaborate with cross-functional teams, and contribute to the development of innovative software solutions.

 

As a Frontend Software Engineer at DummyNode, you'll be at the forefront of crafting seamless user experiences for both web and mobile applications. Your role will involve translating design concepts into responsive and intuitive interfaces, optimizing performance for various mobile platforms, and collaborating closely with designers and backend engineers to ensure a cohesive user experience across all devices. This position offers an exciting opportunity to leave your mark on cutting-edge projects and contribute to the advancement of innovative software solutions.

 

Responsibilities

 

  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to design, develop, and deploy software solutions.
  • Design and develop user interfaces for web and mobile applications.
  • Implement responsive and intuitive user experiences that enhance usability and accessibility.
  • Collaborate with designers and backend engineers to integrate frontend components with backend systems.
  • Optimize application performance for mobile devices, including iOS and Android platforms.
  • Stay up-to-date with the latest frontend development trends and technologies.
  • Conduct code reviews and provide constructive feedback to team members.
  • Troubleshoot and debug frontend issues as needed.

Qualifications

 

Required Qualifications

 

  • Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field.
  • Strong proficiency in frontend development technologies such as HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and modern JavaScript frameworks (e.g., React, Angular, Vue).
  • Experience developing mobile applications for iOS and/or Android platforms using frameworks such as React Native or Flutter.
  • Solid understanding of responsive design principles and mobile-first development practices.
  • Knowledge of web accessibility standards and best practices.
  • Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills.
  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills.

 

Preferred Qualifications

 

  • 5+ years of experience in frontend development with a focus on mobile applications.
  • Experience with cross-platform development frameworks such as React Native or Flutter.
  • Familiarity with mobile design patterns and best practices.
  • Previous experience working in an Agile development environment.

  • Mentorship from experienced professionals in the field.
  • Opportunity to network with industry leaders and professionals.
  • Flexible work hours and remote work options.
